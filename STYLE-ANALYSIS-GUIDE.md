# StyleGenius Personal Style Analysis — Full Guide

## What We Collect (from form)
- Name / Email
- Hair color (from chart — e.g. "Caramel", "Light Ash Blonde")
- Eye color (from chart — e.g. "Azure", "Toasty Topaz")
- Skin tone (from 16 photo options — e.g. "Fair Ivory", "Light Cool")
- Body type (from 6 silhouettes — e.g. "Hourglass", "Rectangle")
- Height (e.g. 165cm / 5'5")
- Selfie (natural light, no makeup/filters)

---

## The Report: 10–12 Pages

### Page 1: Cover
- "Your Personal Style Analysis"
- Their name
- StyleGenius branding
- Clean, luxurious design
- **Good for preview: YES — shows the premium feel**

### Page 2: About You
- Summary of their details: hair, eyes, skin tone, body type, height
- Small version of their selfie (or just text summary)
- "Based on your features, here's what we found:"

### Page 3: Your Color Season
- Their season name (e.g. "Soft Summer", "Light Spring")
- What it means — 2-3 sentences explaining their season
- How their hair, eyes, and skin led to this result
- **Good for preview: YES — the big reveal, most exciting page**

### Page 4: Your Best Colors
- 10-12 color swatches with hex codes and names
- Organized by category: everyday basics, statement colors, accent colors
- "These colors make your skin glow, your eyes pop, and create a harmonious look"

### Page 5: Colors to Avoid
- 6-8 colors that wash them out or clash
- Each with a short explanation WHY (e.g. "Orange pulls out redness in cool skin")
- Not aggressive — framed as "wear these less, not never"

### Page 6: Your Makeup Palette
- Foundation undertone guidance (cool/warm/neutral)
- Lip colors: 3-4 shades (everyday, bold, nude)
- Blush: 2-3 shades
- Eyeshadow: 3-4 shades
- All with color swatches
- **Good for preview: YES — very visual, girls love this**

### Page 7: Your Body Type Guide
- Their body type name + illustration
- What it means for proportions
- Goal: balance/enhance (what to emphasize, what to balance)
- General silhouette guidelines

### Page 8: Best Clothing Styles for Your Body
- Tops: what necklines, sleeves, fits work
- Bottoms: jeans cuts, skirt lengths, trouser styles
- Dresses: best shapes and lengths
- Outerwear: jacket/coat styles that flatter
- Specific examples, not vague advice

### Page 9: Capsule Wardrobe Starter
- 15-20 essential pieces in THEIR colors
- Organized: tops, bottoms, dresses, outerwear, shoes
- Each piece described with color suggestion from their palette
- "Build your wardrobe around these and everything matches"

### Page 10: Outfit Ideas
- 4-6 outfit combinations from the capsule pieces
- For different occasions: casual, work, date night, weekend
- Described clearly so they can recreate them

### Page 11: Shopping Guide
- What to look for when shopping (fabrics, fits, colors)
- Quick reference card: "take this with you when shopping"
- Their top 5 colors as swatches they can screenshot
- Brands/stores that tend to carry their style (optional)

### Page 12: Your Style Cheat Sheet (Summary)
- One-page reference they can save to their phone
- Color season + top 5 colors as swatches
- Body type + 3 key rules
- "Yes" and "Avoid" quick lists
- **Good for preview: YES — shows the practical value**

## Preview Images for Landing Page

Best pages to show as preview (use in Canva, blur/crop parts to tease):

1. **Cover page** — shows premium quality, personalized with a name
2. **Color palette page** — most visual, most exciting, the "wow I want that"
3. **Makeup palette** — super practical, easy to understand value at a glance

### How to create previews:
- Make a complete example report for a fake person in Canva
- Export pages 1, 4, and 6 as images
- Slightly blur the bottom half or add a gradient fade — teaser effect
- Use on PersonalAnalysis.tsx to replace the CSS mockup or alongside it
